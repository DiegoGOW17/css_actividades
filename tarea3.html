Program mission

Our community is full of innovators and technologists who are leveraging their skills to create tools, resources, and events with missions focused on a new blueprint for peace and prosperity. As always, we're inspired by our community - by you - and we're committed to helping bring your impactful ideas to life.

This is what we're committing:

  1. $500,000 in infrastructure credits as well as promotion and publicity for new, not-for-profit projects related to improving health and education, reducing inequality, and spurring economic growth. Apply here.

  2. $50,000 in cash donations to the Hollie's Hub for Good Fund, which supports organizations including: Per Scholas, NPower, Black Girls Code, SocialWorks, Dev Color, All Star Code, and Blacks in Technology Foundation.

  3. An ever-growing directory that helps you find other ways to get involved. Find ideas for joining forces, helping existing projects amplify their efforts, and using DigitalOcean resources to learn new concepts and teach others.
